Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Introduction

Commodity Prices Tracker ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Network Data

The App connects to Yahoo Finance API to fetch real-time gold and silver prices. This includes:

  • API requests for commodity price data
  • Basic network information for connectivity

No Personal Information

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Any personal information whatsoever

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Display current gold and silver prices
  • Provide app functionality
  • Ensure proper network connectivity

Data Storage

The App does not store any personal data on your device or our servers. Price data is fetched in real-time and not persisted.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Yahoo Finance API to fetch commodity price data. We are not responsible for Yahoo's privacy practices.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to our systems.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone under 13 years of age.
